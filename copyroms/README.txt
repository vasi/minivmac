CopyRoms READMEPaul C. Prattwww.gryphel.comApril 3, 2010CopyRoms is a very simple program to write out acopy of the ROM from a Macintosh to an image file.More information is available at:"http://minivmac.sourceforge.net/extras/copyroms.html"The ROM image files that CopyRoms saves arecopyright Apple Computer, and may not beredistributed.--You can redistribute CopyRoms and/or modify it under the termsof version 2 of the GNU General Public License as published bythe Free Software Foundation.
