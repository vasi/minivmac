EgretRom: READMEPaul C. PrattOctober 22, 2010EgretRom is a utility to write out to an image file acopy of the ROM of the Egret microcontroller used tomanage power in some Macintosh models.Further information may be found at"http://minivmac.sourceforge.net/extras/egretrom.html".You can redistribute EgretRom and/or modify it under the termsof version 2 of the GNU General Public License as published bythe Free Software Foundation.
