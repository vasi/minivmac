PMURom: READMEPaul C. PrattNovember 14, 2011PMURom is a utility to write out to an image file acopy of the ROM of the Power Management Unit (PMU) usedin some Macintosh models.Further information may be found at"http://minivmac.sourceforge.net/extras/pmurom.html".You can redistribute PMURom and/or modify it under the termsof version 2 of the GNU General Public License as published bythe Free Software Foundation.
